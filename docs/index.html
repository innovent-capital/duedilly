How It Works Behind the Scenes

1

Read-only access means your files stay yours

Your AI agent connects to your Google Drive, Dropbox, or accounting software with read-only permissions. It can see and analyze your documents, but it can never modify, delete, or move your original files. Your data stays where it is, under your control.

2

Automatic synchronization with key records

Connect your cap table (Carta, Pulley), financial dashboard (QuickBooks, Xero), and KPI tracker. Your data room automatically reflects your latest metrics, ownership structure, and financial performance—no manual updates required.

3

AI anticipates questions and generates answers

By analyzing your documents and comparing them to typical diligence checklists, your agent predicts what investors will ask. It can generate summaries, create missing documents (like cap table rollforwards), and flag potential concerns before anyone else sees them.

4

Verification catches issues before investors do

Your agent cross-references documents for consistency. It checks that your cap table matches your shareholder agreements, that your revenue matches your bank statements, and that all contracts have the required signatures. Fix issues privately, before they become red flags.

5

A living data room that evolves with your business

Unlike static document repositories, your Due Dilly data room stays current. Close a new customer? It's in the metrics. Update your hiring plan? It's in the deck. Sign a new contract? It's validated and categorized. Your data room is always fundraise-ready.

"When your data room answers questions before they're asked, you're not just prepared. You're impressive."
